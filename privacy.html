TipidPayroll — Privacy Policy

Effective: 8 June 2026 · App: TipidPayroll (ph.tipidpayroll.app) · Publisher: MAK-CT Construction OPC

The short version

TipidPayroll works fully offline. Your crews, workers, attendance, pakyaw entries, cash-advance (bale) ledger, and payroll runs stay in your phone's local storage. We cannot see them. There is no account, no sign-up, and no upload of your job data.

Data stored on your device only

  • Crews, worker names/trades/rates, attendance days, pakyaw entries, advances and repayments, closed payroll sheets, letterhead, settings — stored in your browser/app local database (IndexedDB). Deleting the app (or its data) deletes them. The backup file you can export is saved where you choose and never sent to us.

Data you may choose to send us

  • Feedback: if you tap Send in More ▸ Feedback, we receive your message plus anonymous technical context (app version, language, device model string, screen size, record counts, recent error messages). No names, no contact details unless you type them, no job data.
  • Error reports: if "auto-send error reports" is on (you can turn it off in More), the same anonymous technical context may be sent when the app hits an error, so repeat crashes get fixed fast.

Advertising (free tier)

The free Android app shows ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may process your device's advertising identifier and related signals to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can reset or delete your advertising ID in Android settings. TipidPayroll Pro removes ads. The web (PWA) version currently shows no ads.

What we never do

  • No sale of personal data. No contact scraping. No location tracking. No analytics SDKs beyond the ad network in the free Android tier.

Data Privacy Act (PH)

We follow the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). Because your job data never leaves your device, TipidPayroll processes no personal data server-side except the optional feedback described above, which is used solely to improve the app and is deleted when no longer needed.

Children

TipidPayroll is a professional construction tool, not directed at children under 13.

Your workers' information

Names, rates, attendance and advance records you enter about your crew are stored only on your device (and in backup files you export). You are responsible for handling your workers' information fairly; the app never transmits it to us or anyone else.
